About Us

AMK Indoor Turf , established in 2024 by Mr. Abdul Manaf, stands as Thiruvananthapuram's premier indoor sports facility, committed to providing world-class infrastructure for football and cricket enthusiasts. Located in the heart of Ambalathara, we have established ourselves as the go-to destination for both casual players and serious athletes.

Our state-of-the-art facility features professional-grade artificial turf that meets international standards, ensuring optimal playing conditions year-round. We take pride in maintaining the highest safety standards and regularly servicing our turf to provide a consistent, safe playing surface.

Beyond just providing facilities, we are deeply committed to promoting sports culture in Kerala. We regularly host tournaments, provide coaching opportunities, and support local sports development initiatives. Our experienced staff ensures every visitor receives professional service and support.
